Sam Stoller's 81st Birthday Party

On our most recent trip down to LA to see Betty, my dad happened upon the old recorder the family used to record family gatherings. Unfortunately the tapes are in deteriorating conditions and not all of them would play, but we did find a recording of Sam Stoller’s 81st Birthday party that I digitized to keep for posterity and share with all of you.

Highlights of this blast from the past for me include:

  • Hearing Sam Stoller’s voice and one of his famous “speeches” to the family.
  • Hearing Aunt Regina’s voice.
  • Hearing Frank’s toast to Sam Stoller.
  • Hearing Carol singing with her guitar for Aunt Eleanor’s birthday.
  • Hearing one of Uncle Herman’s limericks.
  • Hearing Sam Stoller commenting that Marshall and Jackie are “watching from the sidelines with interest” after detailing everyone else’s many accomplishments.

Enjoy!
