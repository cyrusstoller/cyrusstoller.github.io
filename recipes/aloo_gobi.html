[Recipe]: Aloo Gobi

Jul 01, 2021

  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Prep time: 30 mins
  • Cook time: 30 mins
  • Total time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, peeled and chopped into small pieces
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin seeds
  • 2 teaspoons of tumeric
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 heaping teaspoon of rocoto paste
  • 1 can (28 ounces) of diced tomatoes with juice
  • 1 inch of fresh ginger, peeled and chopped
  • 5 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • 1 large cauliflower
  • 3 large yukon gold potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 2 teaspoons of garam masala
  • 1/4 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 large bunch of cilantro, roughly chopped

Process

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large sauce pan.
  2. Add onion and cumin seeds. Cook until onions become golden and translucent.
  3. Add tumeric and salt.
  4. Add rocoto paste.
  5. Stir tomatoes into onion mixture.
  6. Add garlic and ginger and mix thoroughly.
  7. Add cauliflower and potatoes to the sauce plus a few teaspoons of water. Cover and let simmer for 20 minutes or until potatoes are cooked.
  8. Add garam masala and sugar and stir.
  9. Sprinkle chopped cilantro as garnish.
