[Recipe]: Vegetarian Kimchi Soup

May 29, 2021

  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 30 minutes
  • Total time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 4
  • Adapted from Maangchi kimchi-jjigae

Ingredients

  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 6 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup kimchi (aged, if possible)
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 3 baby bok choy, quartered
  • 1 box of tofu, cut into cubes
  • 1/4 cup of kimchi juice
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 4 tablespoons gochujang
  • 1 tablespoon Korean red pepper flakes (gochugaru) or red chili flakes
  • 1 teaspoon rocoto chili paste
  • 1/4 cup green onion, sliced
  • Cooked rice, for serving
  • Furikake or sesame seeds as garnish

Process

  1. Sautee onion, garlic, and kimchi until onions are translucent.
  2. Add the vegetable stock and bring to a low boil.
  3. Add remaining ingredients and let simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Serve over rice with furikake and/or sesame seeds on top.
