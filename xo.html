What is XO?

XO is for people with friend and actual crushes (e.g. think anything from bromance to romance). It’s all up to you. There are no rules.

Being direct is always the best policy, but if you need a helping hand to reach out to someone you care about, hopefully this is useful. (Colloquially people have called this “the firesale” in the past, but I find that name distasteful, demeaning and misleading.) You may have seen something like this at the end of college. This is exactly what you think.

This is your one wild and precious life. Have at it!

How does it work?

Enter 7 HBS email addresses of your crushes. You only get 7. You can leave some blank if you’d like. Behind the scenes you’ll get an email if you’re both on each others’ lists. If you don’t match, then nothing happens. So, choose carefully.

Is this private?

I’m not going to share this data with anyone unless you’re a match.

What’s the timeline?

The website is live right now! Scroll down to enter your preferences. But, you should probably work your back channel first to make sure that you’re entering people that might enter you too.

Matches will only be computed once at 23:59 EST on Sunday April 30, 2017. You’ll receive an email on Monday May 1, 2017 if you have any matches.

Who can participate?

Technically it’s open to everyone, but you’ll maximize your chances by entering the email addresses of current ECs.

Enter your preferences

You’ll get an email confirming your preferences. Click the link to confirm your identity and you’re all set. If you want to update your preferences, fill out this form again and click confirmation link in the next email you receive.
