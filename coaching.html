Coaching

Thank you for considering me to be your coach. My goal is to help you grow professionally and personally. Often people find sessions helpful when they’re in the midst of a career transition or figuring out what they’d like to do next. Sometimes transitions are between industries, other times they’re as people move from being an individual contributor to a being a manager, and other times they’re as people are commiting themselves to learn a new skill-set (e.g. full-stack web development). If this sounds like you, please reach out so we can figure out whether we’d be a good match for each other.

For more information about me and how I think, please take a look through my blog posts on this website and my 400+ short form posts. These range from more technical tutorials, to leadership philosophy, to ways that I would approach sticky situations.
