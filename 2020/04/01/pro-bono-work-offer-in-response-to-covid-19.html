Pro-bono work offer in response to COVID-19

Many businesses, non-profits, and individuals are suffering economically due to COVID-19. With limited cash flow, organizations must adapt and innovate to survive.

If you work for an organization (large or small) or are an individual with a need for which some software would be helpful, I want to see if I can support you. For example, do you need help putting up a website, writing custom software, or setting up a system to take remote orders?

Please send an email to pro-bono@hoshon.com with the following information and I’ll get back to you as soon as I can about whether I can be of help.

  • Some background on you or your organization
  • A link to your current website or Yelp/Google profile (if you have one)
  • Information about the specific problem that you need help solving

I’m grateful to those who are putting themselves on the front lines to protect our communities during this time. What I have to offer doesn’t compare, but I want to contribute how I can.

Please share this post with anyone who you think could benefit.

I look forward to hearing from you.
